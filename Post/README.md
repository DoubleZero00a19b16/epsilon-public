# Epsilon — LinkedIn Launch Graphics

Three-slide visual campaign introducing **Epsilon** to LinkedIn. Designed in a Swiss/Brutalist editorial style using Epsilon's brand palette (`#FCEAE8` cream · `#1A365D` navy).

---

## Slides

### 1 · Meet the Founders
Introduces the three co-founders photographed at OBA Market. Follows a bold headline + 3-column portrait grid layout.

### 2 · How Epsilon Works
A three-step explainer — **Shop & Rate → AI-Powered Automation → Actionable R&D** — breaking down the platform's core loop in plain language.

### 3 · Building the Future of Retail, Together.
Partnership announcement card featuring the Epsilon × OBA Market integration, backed by the Veysəloğlu Group of Companies.
