About Us

Epsilon is a retail intelligence platform built to transform how retailers understand and respond to customer behavior. Incubated by the Veyseloğlu Group of Companies, we are integrating directly into the OBA Market ecosystem to revolutionize discount retail through data-driven decision-making. Our system operates on a simple yet powerful model: customers connect their loyalty cards to rate products immediately after purchase, earning instant cash rewards while giving previously silent shoppers a voice. This zero-marginal-cost data feeds our AI-powered automation engine, which analyzes user behavior to generate reputation scores and delivers automated recommendations for critical market decisions, turning 100% actionable data into competitive advantage.
